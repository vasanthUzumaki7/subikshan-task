Deputy Director in charge of ESG Control and Monitoring

Ministry of Finance and Public Credit, Mexico

About the Speaker:

Yunuen Hernandez Sunoo is Deputy Director in charge of ESG Control and Monitoring within the Debt Management Office in the Ministry of Finance and Public Credit of Mexico. She participated in the development of the SDG Sovereign Bond Framework, and on the sustainable issuances of thematic bonds with an outstanding amount of 15bn USD as of January 2024. She has participated in the design and development of three SDG Bond Allocation and Impact Reports, on which the latest was published in 2023. Within the Ministry, she has also performed as Deputy Director of Structuring and Projects Analysis, where she provided analysis and financial strategic advice to the National Infrastructure Fund to ensure liquidity and promote infrastructure investment in Mexico. She has participated directly on asset securitizations and liability managements transactions for this fund. She has collaborated in the institutionalization of processes within the DMO regarding thematic debt linked to ESG criteria. She has participated in several fora sharing the Mexican experience in issuing thematic debt, both at a national and international level. She has a bachelor’s degree on Financial Strategy, from the Mexico Autonomous Institute of Technology.
